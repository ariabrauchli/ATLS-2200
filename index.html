One Direction

One Direction are an English-Irish boyband. The group was formed on July 23rd, 2010, on The X Factor in London, England, where they came in third in the competition.

The band consists of five members: Harry Styles, Liam Payne, Niall Horan, Zayn Malik, and Louis Tomlinson.

They quickly rose to fame with their captivating looks and jams, and tickets for their concerts often sold out in minutes.

After Zayn Malik left in March 2015, the rest of the band dropped one more album before going on an indefinite hiatus.
